Personas

 

Persona: Aria, User Group: Traveling Young Adult

  • Aria is a 22 year old university student about to finish his undergrad. As he plans for his grad trip to Asia he hopes to maintain contact with his family and friends back in North America using Facebook Messenger with a Wifi Connection.
  • With travel on his mind Aria is also looking for a method of payment in foreign countries without having to carry around all of his credit cards or his cash
  • Aria can’t stand having to wait at all for his apps to load as he has little patience and attention span
  • Aria is very confident in his technical skills and hates when apps give him Tips
  • Aria struggles to maintain a healthy relationship with his family as he is always away from home and neglects calling home often due to the mediocrity of the conversations

Persona: Bob, User Group: Retiree

  • Bob is a 65 year old Chief of Medicine at a hospital
  • Bob has little experience with new internet phones, with his primary method of contact being his hospital pager which he has used for decades
  • After a patient uses their internet phone to reveal Bob’s real graduation date he takes an interest in learning how they work so that he too may reap the benefits of having access to the internet at all times
  • Bob is largely stubborn and truculent, and wants to maintain the appearance that everything comes easy to him, however, he is willing to secretly put in as much time as it takes to master something he cares about
  • Bob is sensitive about his weight and is looking to lose a few pounds but is unable to resist the urge of muffins that cross his path
