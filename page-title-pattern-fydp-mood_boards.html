Moodboards

1.Light Accents on Dark

2. Simple Ocean

3. Diversified Ocean


















The first moodboard reflects an overall dark atmosphere with vibrant colours. Important information could be relayed using the blood red shown in the bottom left, while the white shown in top right used for neutral information such as settings. Readability may be impeded by overwhelming darkness. This problem can be addressed with changes in the thickness of text and changes in brightness


The second and third moodboards leverage an overall light atmosphere with strong oceanic colours. Important information can be relayed using much darker blues. While light greys can be used for neutral information such as settings. Both of these will result in a strong improvement in readability versus the proposed colour scheme in the previous moodboard. The third moodboard in particular had a heavy influence in the colour scheme of both the first prototype and the refined design
