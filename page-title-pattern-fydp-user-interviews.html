User Interviews

Users

The users are Advertisers using the Ad platform as well as Advertising Firms managing ads for other companies.

User Needs

Both user groups have the following needs:

  • View overall performance of all Ads with performance including the following:
    • # of Impressions
    • $/# Impressions
    • % of Impressions (# of impressions / # of passerbys)
  • View performance of individual ads
  • Be able to break down performance of ads by location and time ranges
  • View store of active and inactive ads
  • Add new ad
  • Delete existing ad
  • Be able to A/B test the performance of two different ads
User Tasks
  • Select ads to serve per location
  • Check in on performance of ads
  • A/B test competing ads in order to serve most effective ones in terms of $/# Impressions
User Preferences
  • Get alerts on ads that are performing poorly relative to other ads in the same location
  • Get suggestions on ads that would do well in a given location based on historical data
User Mental Models
  • Users may have a mental model that the ADAPTV (FYDP project name) ad platform will optimize the serving of their ads in order to maximize impressions
    • This may lead them to putting more effort into the content of their ads
